Features

Everything your
valuables need

Every detail of Travel Vault is designed around one goal: keeping your most important items protected, accessible, and under your control.

Storage

Multi-Compartment Design

Dedicated, padded compartments for passports, cards, cash, and electronics keep everything organized and protected from scratches or bending.

Lock

Smart Locking System

A durable, tamper-resistant lock secures the main compartment. Quick-release access for you, resistance for everyone else.

Access

RFID Authentication

Tap-to-unlock with a paired RFID card or fob. Fast for you, useless to anyone who picks up your pouch.

Alerts

Tamper & Motion Alerts

Built-in sensors detect unauthorized opening attempts or unexpected movement, sending a real-time alert to your connected device.

Carry

Portable, Wearable Design

Lightweight materials and an adjustable strap let you wear Travel Vault under clothing, across your body, or clipped to a bag.

How It Works

Set up once.
Travel protected.

From unboxing to your next departure gate, Travel Vault is ready to go in minutes.

STEP 01

Pair Your Device

Connect Travel Vault to the companion app and pair your RFID key to set up authenticated access.

STEP 02

Pack Your Valuables

Organize your passport, cards, cash, and electronics into the padded compartments designed for each.

STEP 03

Lock & Wear

Seal the secure lock and wear the pouch under clothing, across your body, or attached to your bag.
